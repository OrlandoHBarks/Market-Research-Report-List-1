<p><p>The Polyethersulfone Dialyzer market is categorized into high permeability and low permeability segments based on the membrane's ability to allow solute and fluid transfer. High permeability dialyzers facilitate rapid clearance of larger molecules, making them suitable for patients requiring efficient dialysis treatment. In contrast, low permeability dialyzers are designed for more selective filtration, offering a slower exchange rate, ideal for maintaining specific solute levels in patients with varying clinical needs. Each type caters to distinct therapeutic requirements.</p></p>
