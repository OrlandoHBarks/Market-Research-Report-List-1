<p><p>The electric tailgate system market is categorized into single drive and dual drive types. A single drive system utilizes one motor to automate the opening and closing of the tailgate, offering a cost-effective solution with simplified installation. In contrast, dual drive systems deploy two motors for improved performance, allowing for smoother operation and enhanced stability. Dual drive systems are ideal for larger vehicles, providing additional power and functionality, while single drive systems are suitable for compact designs, prioritizing efficiency and affordability.</p></p>
