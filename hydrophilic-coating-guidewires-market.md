<p><p>The hydrophilic coating guidewires market is segmented into two main types: straight guidewires and elbow guidewires. Straight guidewires are designed for straightforward navigation through vascular pathways, providing ease of insertion and predictable movement. In contrast, elbow guidewires feature a specific curvature that aids in accessing challenging anatomical locations or angles, enhancing maneuverability. Both types utilize hydrophilic coatings to reduce friction, improving navigation through tight or tortuous vessels, thus increasing procedural efficiency and patient comfort during interventions.</p></p>
