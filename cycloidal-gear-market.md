<p><p>Cycloidal gears are a type of speed reducer that utilize a unique mechanism for transforming rotational motion. They feature an eccentric input shaft that drives lobes or pins, which then turn an output shaft at a reduced speed while increasing torque. This design offers advantages such as high efficiency, compactness, and low backlash, making cycloidal gears ideal for various applications like robotics, automotive, and industrial machinery.</p><p>The cycloidal gear market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for automation across various industries.
