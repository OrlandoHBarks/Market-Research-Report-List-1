Electric valve positioners utilize electric signals for precise control, ideal for applications requiring electronic integration. Digital valve positioners incorporate advanced technology, offering enhanced features like diagnostics and communication capabilities, allowing for more accurate control and monitoring of valve performance in complex systems.</p></p>
