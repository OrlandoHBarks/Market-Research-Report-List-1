<p><p>The cardiac bio implant devices market encompasses several critical categories. Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) detect and treat life-threatening arrhythmias. Implantable Cardiac Pacemakers (ICPs) regulate heartbeats for those with irregular rhythms. Heart valves replace or repair damaged valves to restore effective blood flow. Coronary stents maintain open arteries by preventing blockages, while peripheral stents do the same for blood vessels outside the heart. Ventricular Assist Devices support heart function in patients with severe heart failure, enhancing quality of life.</p></p>
