<p><strong>The Wheel Speed Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li><li>Hall Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wheel speed sensors market is primarily categorized into two types: Magnetic Electric Type and Hall Effect Type. Magnetic Electric sensors utilize variable magnetic fields to detect wheel rotation, providing reliable and consistent speed readings. In contrast, Hall Effect sensors leverage magnetic fields to produce an electronic signal when a wheel moves, offering enhanced accuracy and responsiveness. Both types play a crucial role in advanced vehicle safety systems, including anti-lock braking systems (ABS) and traction control, improving overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
