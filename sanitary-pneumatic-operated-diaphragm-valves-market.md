<p><p>The sanitary pneumatic operated diaphragm valves market is categorized into three main types based on connection methods: threaded, welded, and flanged connections. Threaded connection valves are typically easy to install and disassemble, making them suitable for applications with limited space. Welded connection valves offer superior strength and eliminate potential leakage points, ideal for high-pressure environments. Flanged connection valves provide strong, stable connections and facilitate easy maintenance and replacement in larger systems, catering to diverse sanitary processing needs.</p></p>
