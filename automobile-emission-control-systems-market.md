<p><p>The automobile emission control systems market includes various components that reduce harmful emissions. The oxygen sensor monitors exhaust gases to optimize air-fuel mixture, while the EGR valve recirculates exhaust to lower nitrogen oxide emissions. The catalytic converter converts harmful substances into less harmful ones. The air pump introduces fresh air into the exhaust stream to aid combustion, and the PCV valve manages engine crankcase gases. The charcoal canister captures fuel vapors, preventing them from escaping into the atmosphere.</p></p>
