<p><p>Press type welders are essential in the manufacturing sector, primarily categorized into projection welding and spot welding. Projection welding involves creating welds by using pre-formed projections on the workpieces, allowing for precise and strong bonds in applications like automotive parts. Spot welding, on the other hand, involves connecting two metal sheets by applying heat and pressure at specific points, making it ideal for high-volume production in industries such as appliance and automotive manufacturing. Both methods increase efficiency and ensure durability.</p></p>
