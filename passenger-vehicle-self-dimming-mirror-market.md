<p><p>The Passenger Vehicle Self-dimming Mirror market is segmented into two primary types: outer self-dimming mirrors and inside self-dimming mirrors. Outer self-dimming mirrors are installed on vehicle exteriors, automatically adjusting their tint based on ambient light to reduce glare from headlights of trailing vehicles. Inside self-dimming mirrors, located within the cabin, similarly adapt to light conditions to enhance driver visibility and comfort. Both types contribute to improved safety by minimizing distractions and enhancing the overall driving experience.</p></p>
