<p><p>The floating oil skimmers market includes various types designed for efficient oil recovery from water. Belt oil skimmers utilize a moving belt to absorb oil from the surface, making them effective for large-scale spills. Disc oil skimmers, on the other hand, employ rotating discs to collect oil while minimizing water intake. Other types may include specialized skimmers like tube skimmers or suction skimmers, tailored for specific environments or applications. Together, these technologies cater to diverse industrial and environmental needs in oil spill response.</p></p>
