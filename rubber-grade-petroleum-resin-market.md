<p><p>The Rubber Grade Petroleum Resin Market includes three main types: C5 Petroleum Resin, C9 Petroleum Resin, and C5/C9 Petroleum Resin. C5 Petroleum Resin, derived from C5 hydrocarbons, is known for its low molecular weight and excellent compatibility with rubber, enhancing adhesion and flexibility. C9 Petroleum Resin, originating from C9 hydrocarbons, offers superior heat resistance and weatherability, making it ideal for various applications. C5/C9 Petroleum Resin combines features of both, providing balanced performance in various rubber formulations and processing efficiency.</p></p>
