<p><p>The PEX oxygen barrier pipes market consists of various types, including PEX-A, PEX-B, PEX-C, and PEX-AL-PEX pipes. PEX-A pipes, known for their flexibility, are produced using the Engel method, allowing for superior branching capabilities. PEX-B pipes, made via a silane method, offer higher resistance to chemicals and are cost-effective. PEX-C pipes utilize irradiation cross-linking, providing good strength but less flexibility. PEX-AL-PEX pipes, featuring an aluminum layer, combine the benefits of both materials, enhancing durability for heating applications.</p></p>
