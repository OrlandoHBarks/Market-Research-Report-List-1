<p><ul><li>Collectors</li><li>Frothers</li><li>Modifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The froth flotation chemicals market consists of three main types: collectors, frothers, and modifiers. Collectors enhance the attachment of minerals to air bubbles, improving separation efficiency. Frothers generate a stable froth layer, facilitating the rise of bubbles and allowing for effective mineral aeration. Modifiers adjust pH levels and alter surface properties to optimize the flotation process. Together, these chemicals play a crucial role in mineral processing, enhancing recovery rates and overall productivity in mining operations.</p></p>
