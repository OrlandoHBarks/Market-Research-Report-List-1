<p><ul><li>Cationic Antistatic Agent</li><li>Anionic Antistatic Agent</li><li>Nonionic Antistatic Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The durable antistatic agent market comprises three main types: cationic, anionic, and nonionic antistatic agents. Cationic agents, positively charged, effectively neutralize negatively charged surfaces, commonly used in textiles and plastics. Anionic agents, on the other hand, possess a negative charge, suitable for applications in coatings and adhesives. Nonionic agents, which are neutral, offer versatility across various substrates without affecting surface charge, making them popular in personal care and electronic applications. Each type serves distinct needs in controlling static electricity.</p></p>
